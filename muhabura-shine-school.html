Our School

A Community Dedicated to Shining Futures

Muhabura Shine Secondary School was founded on the belief that every child in Kisoro deserves world-class education. Inspired by the enduring spirit of Mt. Muhabura, we empower students to rise above challenges and shine in all they do.

We offer both O-Level (S1–S4) and A-Level (S5–S6) programmes in a nurturing, disciplined, and innovative environment rooted in Ugandan values.

What We Offer

Academic Programs & Curriculum

Our curriculum follows the Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) framework, enriched with skills-based learning and career guidance.

📚

O-Level Programme

Senior 1 to 4. Core subjects including Mathematics, Sciences, Languages, and Social Studies leading to UCE.

🎓

A-Level Programme

Senior 5 and 6. Science, Arts, and Business combinations preparing students for UACE and university.

🔬

Science & STEM

Well-equipped labs for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Hands-on experiments and innovations.

🌍

Humanities & Arts

History, Geography, Economics, Literature, and fine arts fostering critical thinking and creativity.

💻

ICT & Technology

Modern computer lab with internet access. Students gain digital skills vital for the 21st century.

🌿

Agriculture & Environment

Practical agriculture programme leveraging Kisoro's fertile highlands. Environmental stewardship built in.

Our Story

Rooted in Kisoro. Rising to the World.

"Just as Mt. Muhabura stands tall above the horizon, we raise every student to stand tall in life."

Founded to serve the communities of Kisoro District in South-Western Uganda, Muhabura Shine Secondary School has grown into a trusted institution where hundreds of families entrust their children's futures. We combine the warmth of our local community with the ambition of a global outlook — shaping well-rounded, responsible, and capable young Ugandans.

How to Apply

Our admissions process is straightforward. We welcome applications for S1, S5, and mid-year transfers.

  1. Pick up or download the application form
  2. Submit PLE/UCE results and school reports
  3. Sit the school's entrance assessment
  4. Attend interview with parents/guardian
  5. Receive admission letter and pay fees
Request a Form

Fees & Requirements

We offer competitive school fees with flexible payment plans and bursary support for deserving students.

O-Level (S1 – S4)

Day scholars and boarders welcome. Fees per term payable at start of term.

A-Level (S5 – S6)

Science, Arts, and Business combinations. Lab and ICT fees inclusive.
